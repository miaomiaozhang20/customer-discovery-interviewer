This AI tool helps founders and product teams conduct effective customer discovery interviews through a two-stage conversational process. Here's how to get started and make the most of it:

## 🎯 How the Tool Works

### Stage 1: Customer Interview
* You can upload a product or service description (optional - helps provide context)
* The **Interviewer Agent** will begin by asking about the interviewee's background and current situation
* The conversation explores problems, needs, and current behaviors **one theme at a time**
* The interviewer will ask probing questions to get specific examples and details
* **You're in control**: if you prefer not to engage with a question, just say so and the bot will move on
* When you're ready to switch to a new topic, indicate that explicitly to proceed
* The interviewer will ask **three required questions** at the end:
  1. Would you be willing to pay for [product/service]?
  2. What question(s) should I be asking that I haven't included?
  3. Are you willing to be contacted by founders? (If yes, leave your email)
* Use the **"Start Report Writer"** button in the sidebar when ready to move to insights generation

### Stage 2: Insights Report
* The **Report Writer Agent** transforms your interview into a structured insights report
* It analyzes the conversation for patterns, validation signals, and red flags
* Creates a first draft with problem analysis, willingness to pay assessment, and recommendations
* You can request any changes, additions, or refinements until you're satisfied
* Once satisfied, **download your final report as a Word document** using the **Download** button

## 🛠️ Additional Features & Tips
* Click **"Restart"** to begin a completely new interview at any time
* You can **resume a previous session** by clicking **"Load a Past Session"** and selecting from your history
* ⚠️ *We recommend starting from a past session if you accidentally close or refresh your tab*
* If something goes wrong, click **"Try again"** or close the error window with **"X"** and retry
* Persistent issues? Use the **"Report a Bug"** option in the top-right menu

## 🔄 Navigating Between Stages
* Click **"Start Report Writer"** in the left sidebar to move from interview to report generation
* Click **"Return to Interviewer"** to go back to the interview stage
* ⚠️ **Important:** Switching to Report Writer preserves your interview, but returning to Interviewer will delete your current Report Writer chat history

## 💡 Best Practices for Interviewers

### Before the Interview
* **Target the right people**: Interview actual potential customers, not friends or family
* **Prepare context**: Have your product/service description ready (but don't pitch!)
* **Set the tone**: Make it conversational, not interrogative

### During the Interview
* **Stay neutral**: Don't lead the witness or pitch your solution
* **Go deep**: Ask "why" multiple times to get to root causes
* **Demand specifics**: Don't accept vague or hypothetical answers
* **Listen for behavior**: What they've actually done matters more than what they say they'll do
* **Follow the energy**: Probe deeper when they show emotion or enthusiasm
* **One topic at a time**: Fully explore each area before moving on

### What to Listen For

**🚩 Red Flags (Warning Signs):**
* Polite but vague enthusiasm ("That sounds interesting...")
* Lots of future tense ("I would definitely use this...")
* Generic praise without specific value ("People would love this!")
* No current pain point or problem-solving attempts
* Unwillingness to pay despite stated interest
* Answers on behalf of others, not themselves

**✅ Green Flags (Validation Signals):**
* Specific, recent examples with details
* Emotional response when describing problems
* Already spending time/money trying to solve this
* Detailed use cases and scenarios
* Concrete willingness to pay amounts
* Shares contact information for follow-up
* Active problem-solving attempts described

## 💡 Best Practices for Interviewees

### What We're Looking For
* **Honest feedback**: Your truth helps build better products
* **Specific examples**: Share real stories from your experience
* **Current behavior**: What you actually do matters more than what you might do
* **Concrete details**: Numbers, frequency, impact, costs

### How to Participate
* **Be candid**: There are no right or wrong answers
* **Take your time**: Thoughtful responses are valuable
* **Think out loud**: Share your reasoning and thought process
* **Ask questions**: If something is unclear, ask for clarification
* **Stay specific**: Avoid hypotheticals and generalizations

## 📊 What You'll Get

Your insights report will include:
* **Executive Summary**: Key findings at a glance
* **Problem Analysis**: Pain points identified with supporting quotes
* **Current Behavior**: What you're doing now and what you're spending
* **Solution Feedback**: Your reactions to the proposed solution (if discussed)
* **Willingness to Pay**: Analysis of your response and reasoning
* **Validation Signals**: Green flags and red flags identified
* **Recommendations**: Next steps for product development and further research

## 🎓 Customer Discovery Principles

This tool is based on proven customer discovery methodologies including:
* **The Mom Test** by Rob Fitzpatrick
* **Lean Customer Development** by Cindy Alvarez
* **Jobs to Be Done** framework
* **Y Combinator** startup advice

Key principles:
1. **Talk about their life, not your idea**
2. **Ask about specifics in the past, not hypotheticals about the future**
3. **Talk less, listen more**
4. **Focus on behavior, not opinions**
5. **Dig for gold: ask "why" multiple times**
